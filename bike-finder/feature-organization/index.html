Feature Organization

Landing

Users purchasing a bike for the first time lack knowledge about bike features, terrain, and safety. How might we help them learn about bikes while also push them to make an informed purchase?

  • Navigation with generally useful items (categories, search, etc.) and quick access to a diagnostic quiz that will help determine what bike to purchase
  • Landing with a large header/CTA for the bike quiz
  • Below the header, a grid of the most popular bikes for purchase
  • Below popular bikes, articles or FAQ for information about bikes

Quiz

Users purchasing a bike for the first time lack knowledge about bike features, terrain, and safety. How might we help them learn about bikes while also push them to make an informed purchase?

  • A full screen quiz with <10 questions, mostly very simple and straightforward, about use case and other circumstances
  • Upon finishing the quiz, display the recommended bikes and traits the user has indicated. Ensure the user has the ability to modify their quiz answers and then provide links to the recommended bikes or bike types

Bikes

When browsing bikes, users want to be able to see all of their options and customize their results to their liking. How might we enable users to be efficient in their bike search while also providing them with information about the bike they are looking for?

  • Top level categories so users can easily switch
  • A left column with many customization options, each/some option with a hover tooltip to explain what it does
  • List each bike in a X by Y grid, each bike should have a photo, name, brand, price, and potentially, review information, and styles
  • Offer an item on a top level navigation element that allows users to compare bikes

Bike Page

When interested in a bike, users want to know information about the specific bike down to every detail, view different styles and customizations they can make, view shipping and pick-up options, read reviews, and see other bikes/items that are similar to the one they are looking at.

  • A gallery of images of the bike, with each image enlarged when it is focused
  • A right column with price, reviews, styles, size, quantity, shipping, and etc.
  • Information about the bike that is more specific in nature
  • A grid of related bikes/accessories
