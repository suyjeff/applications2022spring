Wireframe Testing

Test 1 with Christine

Since my wireframe was drawn and there is not a lot of placeholder text related to actual bikes, Christine used some of the annotations I had provided. All of the movement through the flow seemed straightforward. The individual bike page was clear enough that Christine could infer what the text scribbles would be without having any real text.

Test 2 with Kathleen

Kathleen had similar comments as Christine. I think the lack of placeholder text may be hurting the effectiveness of some of the walkthroughs. Aside from that, the flows and screens are clear enough to understand for specific personas.

Test 3 with Christina

Similar to the first test, we were able to parse through the flows pretty quickly. When analyzing my wireframes for a user looking for a safe study bike, she determined that, if comprehensive enough, the quiz would likely lead her to the proper bikes.
